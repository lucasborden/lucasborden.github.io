About Me

Welcome! I’m Lucas Borden, a predoctoral research associate in economics at the University of Zurich working with Professors Dina Pomeranz and Ronak Jain. My research interests lie at the intersection of behavioral and development economics. More recently, I have been delving into the world of public finance.

Before joining the University of Zurich, I earned an M.Sc. in Development Economics from the University of San Francisco and a B.A. in Economics from Boston College.
